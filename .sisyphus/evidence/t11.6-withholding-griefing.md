## Critical/High Fixes Applied

### C1 — `aggregate_decrypt` duplicate party_id

**File:** `crates/pvthfhe-fhe/src/mock_impl.rs`

**RED test** (`t11_6_aggregate_decrypt_rejects_duplicate_party_id`): confirmed failing before fix (returned `Ok` when same share submitted twice to meet threshold).

**Fix:** Added `BTreeSet` deduplication of `party_id` before threshold count, returning `Err(FheError::MalformedDecryptShare { party_id })` on first duplicate.

**GREEN:** test passes post-fix.

---

### C2 — `aggregate_keygen` duplicate party_id

**File:** `crates/pvthfhe-fhe/src/mock_impl.rs`

**RED test** (`t11_6_aggregate_keygen_rejects_duplicate_party_id`): confirmed failing before fix (returned wrong key silently).

**Fix:** Added `BTreeSet` deduplication of `party_id` before XOR accumulation, returning `Err(FheError::MalformedKeygenShare { party_id })` on first duplicate.

**GREEN:** test passes post-fix.
