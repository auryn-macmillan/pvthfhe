## Reviewer Feedback Disposition

The following workflow governs how feedback from external reviewers is processed and integrated into the program.

### Feedback Classification
1.  **Blocking**: Identification of critical security flaws, soundness issues, or fundamental theoretical errors.
2.  **Advisory**: Suggestions for performance optimization, improved documentation, or minor technical refinements.

### Processing Workflow
*   **Blocking Issues**: Trigger an immediate pause on the affected workstream. The project team must draft a resolution plan. A follow-up review is required to clear the blocking status. Blocking issues may necessitate a plan change or pivot as per the Program Charter.
*   **Advisory Issues**: Logged in the project issue tracker. The team assesses the impact versus complexity. Adoption is at the team's discretion, but each advisory point must receive a response in the feedback log.
*   **Final Authority**: The Project Lead holds final decision-making authority on the disposition of all feedback. In cases of disagreement between reviewers and the team, the Lead's determination is final.

### Logistics
All reviews are documented using the `reviewer-memo-template.md` and saved to `.sisyphus/reviews/`.
