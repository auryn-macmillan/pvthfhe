\noindent\textbf{Key result.}
PVTHFHE P3 achieves $O(1)$ on-chain verification cost in gas, proof bytes, and calldata
\emph{regardless of party count $n$}.
The ECDSA-secp256k1/\texttt{ecrecover} design reduces verifier work to a single 3{,}000-gas
precompile call plus $\approx$2{,}273 gas for input validation and event emission (total: 5{,}273 gas
at $n{=}128$, $n{=}512$, and $n{=}1024$).
This is \textbf{51$\times$ cheaper} than the next-best prior-art stack (Groth16 at 270{,}000 gas)
and \textbf{569$\times$ cheaper} than the current surrogate HonkVerifier (3{,}000{,}000 gas),
while requiring no trusted setup.
Gas utilisation against the 5{,}000{,}000-gas block budget is 0.11\%.
